Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

1. Data We Collect

Spendly collects personal financial information that you voluntarily enter into the application. This includes expense amounts, categories, dates, descriptions, and any notes you add to your transactions. We do not collect or store your bank account credentials, credit card numbers, or other sensitive financial account information.

2. How We Use Your Data

The information you provide to Spendly is used solely for the purpose of helping you track and analyze your personal expenses. Your data is used to generate spending summaries, category breakdowns, and other financial insights within the application. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal financial data with third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your expense data is stored locally within the application. We recommend regularly backing up your financial information. While we implement reasonable security measures to protect your data, you are responsible for maintaining the security of your device and account credentials. Spendly is not liable for data loss resulting from device failure, unauthorized access, or other circumstances beyond our control.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not integrate with external banking services, payment processors, or other third-party financial platforms. Your data remains within the application and is not transmitted to external services. We do not use third-party analytics or advertising services that collect user data.

5. Cookies and Tracking

Spendly uses essential cookies to maintain your session and ensure the proper functioning of the application. We do not use tracking cookies, advertising pixels, or other technologies that monitor your browsing behavior across other websites.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. If we make material changes, we will notify you through the application or by email. Your continued use of Spendly after the effective date of any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us. We are committed to addressing your privacy concerns and ensuring the responsible handling of your personal financial information.
